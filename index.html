So what is really happening?

As you know, calls and text messages all go through cell towers. During disasters, cell towers get overwhelmed with traffic and go down. What we're doing is making sure that the important stuff can go through by dropping less essential traffic.
This page is our demo. The control panel on the left lets you send traffic towards a cell tower that we built. The charts above show what kinds of data we receive and how many we actually serve. The calls/texts/searches that aren't served are dropped so our cell tower doesn't get overwhelmed.
